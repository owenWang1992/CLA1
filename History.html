History of Pure Milk Tea:

Pure Milk Tea was first founded in 1950 in Bejing. It has more than 60 years history.
In the 1960s, the founder Mr.Wang innovated his own formula and way to cook milk tea which very attracted many young Chinese people.
Later, Mr.Wang extended his brand Pure Milk Tea to all state in China and added more options like snacks to the manu.
In the begin of 21 century, Pure Milk Tea was becoming one of the most popular boba shop in China.
Now, Mr.Wang wants to extend his brand to foreign countries like Janpan, South Korea and the U.S.
